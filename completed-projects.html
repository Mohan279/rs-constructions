Completed Projects

Explore our successfully delivered construction projects

Green Valley Shopping Mall

Location: Green Valley
Area:500,000 sq.ft
Completed: January 2024

The Green Valley Shopping Mall is a state-of-the-art retail complex featuring 200 premium retail spaces, a multiplex cinema, food court, and entertainment zones. The project includes advanced security systems, energy-efficient lighting, and smart parking solutions. The mall's modern architecture with glass facades and open spaces creates an inviting shopping experience. Special features include a central atrium, children's play area, and dedicated spaces for events and promotions.

Lakeside Apartments

Location: Lakeside
Area:300,000 sq.ft
Completed: November 2023

Lakeside Apartments is a premium residential complex featuring 150 luxury units of 2, 3, and 4 bedrooms with stunning lake views. The project includes modern amenities such as a swimming pool, fitness center, clubhouse, and landscaped gardens. Each apartment is designed with spacious layouts, high-end finishes, and smart home technology. The complex also features secure parking, 24/7 security, and dedicated maintenance staff. The architecture blends contemporary design with natural elements, creating a serene living environment by the lake.

Tech Hub Office Park

Location: Tech District
Area:400,000 sq.ft
Completed: August 2023

Tech Hub Office Park is a modern business complex designed for technology companies and startups. The project features 10 interconnected buildings with flexible office spaces, collaborative work areas, and state-of-the-art conference facilities. Each building is equipped with high-speed internet, smart building systems, and sustainable energy solutions. The campus includes a central plaza, food court, fitness center, and outdoor meeting spaces. The architecture emphasizes natural light and open spaces, creating an innovative work environment that fosters creativity and productivity.

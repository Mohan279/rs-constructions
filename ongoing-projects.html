Ongoing Projects

Take a look at our current construction projects

Downtown Office Complex

Location: Downtown Jabel Ali
Progress: 65% Complete
Expected Completion: Q4 2024

The Downtown Office Complex is a landmark development featuring three interconnected towers with premium office spaces, retail areas, and conference facilities. The project includes advanced building management systems, sustainable energy solutions, and smart security features. Each tower offers panoramic city views, natural lighting, and flexible floor plans. The complex will feature a central plaza, underground parking, and direct access to public transportation. The modern architecture with glass and steel facades will create an iconic presence in the downtown skyline.

Riverside Residential Tower

Location: Dubai Design District
Progress: 45% Complete
Expected Completion: Q4 2024

The Riverside Residential Tower is a 35-story luxury development offering stunning river views and premium living spaces. The tower features 200 luxury apartments ranging from 2 to 4 bedrooms, each with high-end finishes and smart home technology. Residents will enjoy exclusive amenities including a rooftop infinity pool, riverside promenade, state-of-the-art fitness center, and a sky lounge. The project incorporates sustainable features such as solar panels, rainwater harvesting, and energy-efficient systems. The design emphasizes natural light and ventilation, with floor-to-ceiling windows and private balconies in every unit. Special features include 24/7 security, concierge services, and electric vehicle charging stations.

Tech Park Expansion

Location: Ras Al Kor
Progress: 30% Complete
Expected Completion: Q1 2025

The Tech Park Expansion is a state-of-the-art development featuring five new office buildings, research facilities, and innovation hubs. The project includes advanced infrastructure with high-speed internet, smart building systems, and sustainable energy solutions. Each building offers flexible workspaces, collaborative areas, and cutting-edge conference facilities. The expansion will feature a central innovation plaza, food court, fitness center, and outdoor meeting spaces. The architecture emphasizes natural light and open spaces, creating an environment that fosters creativity and technological advancement. Special features include electric vehicle charging stations, green spaces, and dedicated areas for tech startups.

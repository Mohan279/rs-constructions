Tech Park Development

Location: Dubai International City
Area: 70,000 sq.m
Start Date: Q4 2024

The Tech Park Development will be a state-of-the-art technology campus featuring 10 modern office buildings, research facilities, and innovation hubs. The project will include advanced infrastructure with high-speed internet, smart building systems, and sustainable energy solutions. Each building will offer flexible workspaces, collaborative areas, and cutting-edge conference facilities. The campus will feature a central innovation plaza, food court, fitness center, and outdoor meeting spaces. The architecture emphasizes natural light and open spaces, creating an environment that fosters creativity and technological advancement. Special features include electric vehicle charging stations, green spaces, and dedicated areas for tech startups.

Luxury Residential Tower

Location: Dubai Design District
Area: 28,000 sq.ft
Start Date: Q4 2024

The Luxury Residential Tower will be a 40-story architectural masterpiece offering panoramic views of the waterfront. The development will feature 200 luxury apartments ranging from 2 to 4 bedrooms, each with premium finishes and smart home technology. Residents will enjoy exclusive amenities including a rooftop infinity pool, private beach access, state-of-the-art fitness center, and a sky lounge. The tower will incorporate sustainable features such as solar panels, rainwater harvesting, and energy-efficient systems. The design emphasizes natural light and open spaces, with floor-to-ceiling windows and private balconies in every unit.

Community Center

Location: Dubai International City
Area: 14,000 sq.m
Start Date: Q1 2025

The Community Center will be a modern, multi-purpose facility designed to serve as a hub for social, cultural, and recreational activities. The project includes a large auditorium, multipurpose halls, sports facilities, and outdoor spaces. The center will feature sustainable design elements, including solar panels, rainwater harvesting, and energy-efficient lighting. The architecture incorporates open spaces and natural light to create an inviting atmosphere. Special features include a library, computer lab, art studio, and spaces for community events and workshops.
